## Ideal Pilot User Profile

Looking for people who:

- Work with CSV or Excel data regularly (at least weekly)
- Produce reports or summaries for clients, managers, or stakeholders
- Currently spend meaningful time on data cleaning or formatting
- Are not blocked by enterprise procurement / IT security review
- Can commit 4 weeks and 30 minutes of honest feedback

**Best-fit roles:**
- Freelance / independent data analysts
- Business or management consultants
- Marketing analytics consultants
- Accounting and finance analysts who produce client deliverables
- Operations analysts at small companies (<50 people)
- Research analysts at agencies or nonprofits

**Local first:** Winnipeg-area consultants and business analysts are ideal for an in-person demo or quick follow-up call.

## Outreach Positioning

**One-line pitch:**
> "Upload a dataset, get a client-ready report in minutes — cleaning, findings, and export included."

**Key differentiators to mention:**
- No code required
- Every cleaning step is auditable before you sign off
- Report Builder lets you choose which findings go to the client
- Export to HTML or Excel — works in any client meeting

**What NOT to claim:**
- Do not say "enterprise-grade" or "SOC 2 compliant" — it is not
- Do not say "AI replaces your analysis" — it augments it
- Do not promise PDF export is always available (environment-dependent)

## Pilot Call Agenda (30 minutes)

| Time | Topic |
|------|-------|
| 0–2 min | Intro — who they are, what kind of data work they do |
| 2–5 min | Their current workflow — how do they go from data to client deliverable today? |
| 5–20 min | Live demo or screen share of their trial session — what did they use? what happened? |
| 20–27 min | Structured feedback (see questions below) |
| 27–30 min | Next steps — would they pay? what would make them recommend it? |

**Feedback questions to ask:**
1. What part of the workflow saved you the most time?
2. Was there anything confusing or broken?
3. Would you feel confident sharing the exported report with a real client?
4. What's missing that would make this a must-have?
5. What would you pay for this per month? (Consultant plan: unlimited workspaces, AI features, exports)
6. Who else in your network would benefit from this?

## Qualification Checklist

Before booking a demo, confirm:

- [ ] They work with CSV, Excel, or structured data at least weekly
- [ ] They produce reports or summaries for someone else (client, manager, stakeholder)
- [ ] They are not blocked by enterprise IT procurement
- [ ] They can realistically test it within 4 weeks
- [ ] They are willing to give honest critical feedback (not just positive)
- [ ] File sizes are within range (<100 MB per file is fine for Consultant plan)

## Red Flags / Bad-Fit Users

| Signal | Why it's a bad fit |
|--------|-------------------|
| "We need SSO / SAML" | Not supported yet — enterprise compliance is not pilot scope |
| "Our IT team needs to approve the tool" | Procurement cycle is too long for a 4-week pilot |
| "We process millions of rows daily" | Current scale is consultant-grade, not data-warehouse scale |
| "Can it connect to our database directly?" | File upload only — no live DB connections in current version |
| "We need a white-label version" | Not available; product is Analyst Pro branded |
| "Does it comply with GDPR / HIPAA?" | Cannot claim compliance yet — advise against uploading regulated data |
